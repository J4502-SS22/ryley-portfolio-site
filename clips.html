Maddy Ryley, Journalist

Interested in B2B publications, pop culture writing, editing

I’m Maddy Ryley, and I am interested in magazine writing, editing and publishing. I have experience writing for both the Missourian and Vox magazine during my years at the University of Missouri’s School of Journalism. I also have been working at Trade Show Executive magazine since May 2020.

I am interested in doing freelance work that focuses more on pop culture and music, while I simultaneously work in the business-to-business publication sphere as my first job. You can check out my projects, resume and send me a message!
