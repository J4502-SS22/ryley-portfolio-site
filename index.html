Maddy Ryley, Journalist

Interested in B2B publications, pop culture writing, editing

I’m Maddy Ryley, and I am interested in magazine writing, editing and publishing. I have experience writing for both the Missourian and Vox Magazine during my years at the University of Missouri’s School of Journalism. I also have been working at Trade Show Executive magazine since May 2020 as an editorial intern.

Following graduation in May of 2022, I will be joining the Trade Show Executive staff full time as its Associate Editor.
