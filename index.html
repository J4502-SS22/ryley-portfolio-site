Maddy Ryley, Journalist

Interested in B2B publications, pop culture writing, editing

I’m Maddy Ryley, and I am interested in magazine writing, editing and publishing. I have experience writing for both the Missourian and Vox Magazine during my years at the University of Missouri’s School of Journalism. In the fall of 2021, I was on Vox's staff as an Eat+Drink editor. I also have been working at Trade Show Executive magazine since May 2020 as an editorial intern, working in the office during the summers and remotely during the school year.

Following graduation in May of 2022, I will be joining the Trade Show Executive staff full time as its Associate Editor.
